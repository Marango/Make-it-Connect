Create It Lab is a volunteer organization working in conjunction with IBM’s Technical Education Outreach Program. The organization’s mission is to provide an environment that facilitates innovation for K-12 students as they explore physical science by building developmentally-appropriate, motivational projects at minimal cost.

Emphasis is placed on the Motivate-Make-Measure-Model design methodology which provides the framework for creativity and empowers students by teaching fabrication skills. This philosophy was originally conceived by Professor Ronald Goldner at Tufts University.

In 2011, the volunteer efforts of Create It Lab in Vermont provided the basis for both a $1500 IBM Community Grant for the Essex High School Fab Lab, as well as a $10000 IBM Community Impact Grant to enhance the STEM curriculum for the Chittenden Central Supervisory Union school district.

Create It Lab volunteers also received 5 United Way Building Block Awards for their involvement in STEM education during 2012.
